Clinical Factors Associated with Renal Outcome After Heart Transplantation. Cardiorenal syndrome (CRS) frequently occurs in end-stage heart failure patients waiting for heart transplantation (HT). Decision-making regarding simultaneous heart and kidney transplantation is an unresolved issue in these patients. We investigated clinical factors associated with renal outcome after HT. A total of 180 patients who received HT from 1996 to 2015 were included.
