The historical odyssey of cardiac transplantation. Often taken for granted, the journey leading to the therapeutic reality of cardiac transplantation was paved by many obstacles and a myriad of successes rooted primarily in the basic principle of perseverance. One cannot pretend to understand the sensational evolution of cardiac transplantation without a look back at the historical accomplishments concurrently achieved across a variety of disparate disciplines in medicine, surgery, and immunology that heralded contemporary outcomes. This historical review will delineate landmark events in the field of cardiac transplantation, a procedure once considered a "fantastic speculation for the future."
