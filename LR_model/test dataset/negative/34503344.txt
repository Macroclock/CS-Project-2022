Lay abstract Cardiac transplantation is the gold standard for treatment for select patients with end-stage heart failure, but the supply of viable donor organs is limited. New technology known as machine perfusion is now capable of preserving donor hearts outside the body and can be used to assess the donor organ as well as allow transport over longer distances.
