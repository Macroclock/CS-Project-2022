Acute and long-term efficacy and safety of sildenafil for the treatment of chronic thromboembolic pulmonary hypertension in a heart transplant recipient. We report on the successful treatment with sildenafil of a unique case of severe chronic thromboembolic pulmonary hypertension which developed as a late complication in a 71-year-old heart transplant recipient, with focus on the potential therapeutic challenges encountered in the management of such a peculiar association of clinical conditions.
