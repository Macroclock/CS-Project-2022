Heart Transplantation With Pulmonary Vein Reconstruction in Ewing's Sarcoma in the Right Ventricular Outflow Tract. Ewing's sarcoma is a histological type of malignant mesenchymal tumor and rarely originates from the heart. We report a case of Ewing's sarcoma located in the right ventricular outflow tract that invaded the left pulmonary vein in a 29-year-old female.
