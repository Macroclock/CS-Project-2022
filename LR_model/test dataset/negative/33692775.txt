Case Report: Early Transplant Rejection of a Methanol-Intoxicated Donor Heart in a Young Female Patient. A Diagnostic Approach With CMR, Cardiac Biopsy, and Genetic Risk Assessment. This case report describes the contributions of multimodality imaging, cardiac biopsy, and genetic sequencing to the diagnosis and management of heart transplant rejection in a 23-year old patient with dilated cardiomyopathy.
