Echocardiographic diagnosis and management of bilateral coronary ostial atresia in a patient with pulmonary atresia and intact ventricular septum. Bilateral atresia of the coronary artery ostia in patients with pulmonary atresia and intact ventricular septum is a rare condition with no reported survivors beyond early infancy. We report the first case of diagnosis by echocardiography followed by successful cardiac transplantation.
