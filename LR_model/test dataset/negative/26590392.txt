Epstein-Barr futile myocarditis requiring urgent orthotopic heart transplantation. We present the case of a 37-year-old man who was diagnosed with an Epstein-Barr futile myocarditis. The diagnosis was made in the Accident and Emergency Department, with the input of portable echocardiography. The patient subsequently underwent an urgent orthotopic heart transplantation and he has now completely recovered. 'failure', 'futile myocarditis', 'heart', 'transplantation', 'viral'
