[Heart transplantation after corrective surgery of a congenital heart disease. Surgical technical problems]. Heart transplantation was performed in a 17-year old boy with severe left ventricular failure. Previously a congenital heart disease, tetralogy of Fallot was reconstructed in one and half years old of age. Authors present the different surgical problems complicated this procedure. In Hungary this is the first heart transplantation after a corrective surgical procedure of a complex congenital heart disease.
