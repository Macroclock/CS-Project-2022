Diarrhea-An uncommon presentation of tertiary adrenal insufficiency following heart transplantation. Diarrhea following organ transplantation is usually associated with infection and immunosuppression therapy. We describe two patients with diarrhea following orthotopic heart transplantation due to tertiary adrenal insufficiency.
