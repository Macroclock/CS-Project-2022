Late acute aortic dissection following cardiac transplantation in lupus-affected donor aorta. We report the case of a young woman who underwent cardiac transplantation from systemic lupus erythematosus affected donor and who developed a type A aortic dissection limited only to the graft aortic wall 9 years after.
